Mental Health

Mental health encompasses emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act, and how we handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions. Maintaining good mental health is crucial for leading a balanced and fulfilling life.

Understanding Mental Health

What is Mental Health?

  • Mental health encompasses emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how individuals think, feel, and act, and influences how they handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Good mental health is crucial for overall well-being.

Mental Health Disorders

  • Mental health disorders include conditions such as anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia. These disorders can affect anyone and may require professional treatment including therapy, medication, or lifestyle changes.

Fun Facts

  • 1. Around 1 in 5 adults in the U.S. experience mental illness each year.
  • 2. Exercise can be as effective as medication for some people with depression.
  • 3. Talking to friends or family can significantly improve mental health.
